We see you when you're sleeping, we know you're not awake.



Thanks to the @Calpolysleeps Instagram account, Cal Poly students can share and laugh at funny pictures of their fellow students falling asleep on campus. By directly messaging the anonymous account holder, any Cal Poly student can try to make a napping student or faculty member become the next post or Insta story on @Calpolysleeps.
